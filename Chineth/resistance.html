We can have all the knowledge in the world, but it is useless without political power.

What is the point of creating art, practicing resistance in China in secrecy? What is the point of resistance if it automatically censored by Chinese APIs?

We who live in America, who think we can do more: What is the point of resistance that cannot enter the place it is meant to resist? Reach the people it is meant to impact?

A resistance that does nothing and reaches no one is no better than fictional. At least fiction can be told in China.

Is this pseudo-resistance all we can do?

This is our manifesto.
